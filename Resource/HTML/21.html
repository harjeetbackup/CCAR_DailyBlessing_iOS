Baruch shek’raanu
l’romeim goy bitzdakah kakatuv:
V’dirshu et sh’lom ha-ir…
v’hitpal’lu vaadah el Adonai,
ki vish’lomah yihyeh lachem shalom.

Praised be the One who has called us to exalt our nation with righteousness, and taught us: “Seek the welfare of your community and pray on its behalf, so that all may share in its well-being” (Jeremiah 29:7).
