Oseh shalom bimromav, hu yaaseh shalom, aleinu v’al kol Yisrael, v’al kol yoshvei teiveil, v’imru amen.

May the Source of peace grant peace to us, to all Israel, and to all the world.

Adonai oz l’amo yiten, Adonai yivarech et amo va’shalom.

May Adonai grant strength to our people. May Adonai bless our people with peace.
