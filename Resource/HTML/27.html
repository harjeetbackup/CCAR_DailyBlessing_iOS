We give thanks for the gift of children.

Baruch atah, Adonai Eloheinu,
Melech haolam,
shenatan lanu banim y’karim
uvanot y’karot lismoach bahem.

Praise to You, Adonai our God, Sovereign of the universe: You give us precious children in whom we rejoice.

Response:

Baruch shenatan lanu
horim ohavim lismoach bahem.

Praised be the One who gives us loving parents in whom we rejoice.
